## Overview

Led a comprehensive revamp of Domino's train ordering logistics, consisting of two major milestones: the fundamental architectural migration to the NextGen Cart, and the high-impact integration with Zomato IRCTC. Together, these spanning goals modernized the checkout experience and significantly extended delivery reach across India's rail network.

## NextGen Cart Migration

- **Cart Architecture Overhaul**: Completely refactored cart state management to handle complex prerequisites like PNR validation and strict station delivery timings.
- **Advanced Capabilities**: Unlocked targeted promotions and enhanced cross-selling tailored specifically for train journeys, drastically reducing drop-offs in low-connectivity areas.

## Zomato IRCTC Integration

- **Third-Party Extensibility**: Engineered an optimized API template system to seamlessly integrate Domino's ordering ecosystem directly into the Zomato app.
- **Reliable Handoffs**: Designed an end-to-end processing pipeline that synchronized cross-platform status updates in real-time, handling massive traffic spikes.

## Impact

- Boosted the IRCTC flow conversion rate by an impressive **230 bps**.
- Unlocked a massive new acquisition channel via Zomato, driving a **25%** growth in overall train order volume and expanding the brand footprint nationwide.
