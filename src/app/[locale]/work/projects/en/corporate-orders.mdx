## Overview

Led the full backend design and development to digitize Corporate Orders end-to-end. This transformed a fully offline process into a modern self-serve online platform.

## Key Features

- **Automated Workflows**: Streamlined the approval and processing of bulk B2B orders.
- **Self-Serve Portal**: Enabled corporate clients to manage their orders independently.

## Impact

- **Eliminated manual fulfillment overhead**.
- **Expanded B2B revenue** by reducing friction in the ordering process.
